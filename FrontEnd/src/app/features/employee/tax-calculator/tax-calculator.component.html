Instructions

How to use

  • 1. Choose the type of tax estimate you want.
  • 2. Select ESOP or RSU. RSU always uses $0 exercise price.
  • 3. Fetch the latest market price or enter it manually.
  • 4. Enter the number of shares and sale price for selling scenarios.
  • 5. Click Calculate to see tax and net proceeds.

Additional details

Exercise tax is based on the perquisite value of ESOP/RSU exercise.

Selling tax uses LTCG if holding is 12+ months, else slab-based STCG.
