FAQ

Common Tax Scenarios

I received an ISO and NSO - are they taxed differently?

Yes. ISOs receive preferential tax treatment if held long enough, while NSOs are subject to ordinary income tax at exercise. Track them separately and consult with a tax advisor about timing and strategy.

What if my company stock price drops significantly?

For options, an underwater award has no immediate tax impact. For RSUs, you still pay taxes at vesting based on the FMV at vesting date. You can claim capital losses when you sell below your cost basis.

Can I deduct my trading losses?

Yes, capital losses can offset capital gains, and up to $3,000 of net capital losses can offset ordinary income annually. Excess losses can carry forward indefinitely. Consult your tax professional.

How does moving to a different state affect my taxes?

Different states have different tax rates and rules for equity compensation. Your tax liability may change based on where you worked when the award was granted or when you exercised. Consult a tax professional in your new state.
